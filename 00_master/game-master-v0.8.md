# Who Took It? — Game Master v0.8

## Core Identity

**Who Took It?** is a THC — Teaching Healthy Cultivation mystery deduction game.

Players solve one hidden mystery:

> **[Suspect] took [Item].**

## Fixed Missing Items

- Bag
- Dabs
- Lighter
- Chocolate Bar
- Gummies

## Main Rule

Every clue and approved question must resolve to a clear yes/no answer. No maybe answers and no subjective interpretation.

## Supported Modes

### 2-Player Duel Mode
Each player secretly draws one Suspect Card and one Missing Item Card. Each player answers questions about their own hidden mystery. First correct accusation wins. Wrong accusation loses the round.

### 3+ Shared Mystery Mode
One host, app, or answer keeper creates a shared mystery. Players or teams take turns asking yes/no questions. First correct accusation wins.

### Team Mode
Teams share one board and one elimination sheet. Teams may discuss before asking or accusing.

### Single-Player Mode
Single-player is best as a digital mode. The app secretly chooses one suspect and one item, then answers preset yes/no questions using binary trait data.

## Public Board Rule

Public board tiles show only:

1. Character name
2. Portrait/art
3. Three clue tags

Internal primary/secondary item lanes are for design balance only and should not be printed on the final board.
